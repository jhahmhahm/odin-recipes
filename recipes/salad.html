Enjoy a fresh garden salad complemented with a soy vinegrette that delivers a kick.


Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Spinach
  • 2 Cups Chopped Romaine Lettuce
  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup Rice Vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Chili Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Red Pepper Flake
  • Any other Salad toppings you like (Croutouns, Cheese, Tomatoes, Onion, Cucumber)

Directions

  1. Chop the Romain lettuce and place into your bowl with the Spinach
  2. With a small to medium sized bowl, add in your Soy Sauce, Rice Vinegar, Sesame Oil, Chili Oil, and Red Pepper Flakes
  3. Stir your dressing until well mixed
  4. Top your Salad with the dressing and any other toppings you like. Enjoy!
